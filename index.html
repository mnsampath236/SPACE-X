Commercial Real Estate Consulting Firm

Space-X Global Realtors is a Independent real estate consulting firm that focuses primarily on commercial real estate. We do not claim to be the largest, but we do strive to be the best in every transaction we complete. Our goal is to create a win-win situation for all parties involved, not just a successful transaction.

Manish Sharma, the founder of Space-X Realtors & Member of RICS has over 25 years of experience working with clients in a variety of verticals, including office space, hotels and resorts, strip malls, restaurants, and healthcare facilities. He has a proven track record, skill, and reputation in the property and business community.

In a competitive environment, Space-X Realtors assists clients in identifying opportunities, mitigating risk, and maximizing returns. To achieve the solutions, we work as an integrated team across various business disciplines in the real estate consulting business, such as commercial, retail, and industrial.
